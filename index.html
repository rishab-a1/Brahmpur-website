Khel Se Shiksha

Integrated Sports & Education Development Program

Total Budget: ₹15,00,000 | Duration: 12 Months

About Objectives Components Budget Impact Gallery

Executive Summary

This initiative aims to promote sports excellence along with academic support for 250 underprivileged youth (Age 10–18 years). The program combines structured football training, academic coaching, life skills and career guidance.

About the Organization

  • Football training & grassroots sports promotion
  • Youth development programs
  • Academic support initiatives
  • Community sports awareness

Problem Statement

  • Limited access to professional sports coaching
  • Lack of infrastructure
  • Poor academic guidance
  • Risk of school dropout
  • Limited exposure to sports careers

Project Objectives

  • Provide professional football training to 250 students
  • Structured academic support classes
  • Improve discipline & physical fitness
  • Enhance academic performance by 70%
  • Create pathways for scholarships & tournaments

Project Components

1. Football & Sports Training

  • Professional coaching
  • Fitness & endurance training
  • Inter-school tournaments
  • Talent identification camps

2. Infrastructure Development

  • Ground improvement
  • Sports kits & equipment

3. Academic Support

  • After-school coaching
  • Study materials
  • Scholarship exam prep

4. Life Skills & Career Guidance

  • Leadership workshops
  • Nutrition & psychology sessions
  • Career counseling
